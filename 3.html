Become a Mentor

Sign-ups to become a 2015-2016 mentor will be opened in Spring 2015!

There are three requirements that you must meet in order to become a mentor:

  1. You must have a cumulative GPA over 3.0
  2. You must be attending UCLA for the entire 2015-2016 academic year
  3. Attend a one-time training seminar and meet your mentees! Free mentorship t-shirts will be distributed.
