Who We Are? Meet The Board!

We listen, we discuss, we advise and develop. We love to learn and use the latest technologies.

Samantha Tran

Director

Hi everyone! I'm Samantha, the Director for MentorSEAS. I'm a third year electrical engineering major and am currently involved in MentorSEAS, IEEE, and SWE. Thank you for visiting the MentorSEAS website! If you're a current UCLA student then please consider signing up as a mentor during spring quarter, If you're a prospective student then WELCOME! Feel free to email me if you have any questions.

Cheuk Yu

Senior Advisor

My name is Cheuk and I am a four-year Electrical Engineering student leaning towards to the Signal Processing Area. I like the idea of mentorship and have enjoyed my experience of being a student mentor, the mentorship chair of ESUC and HKN, as well as the director of this program. Outside of engineering, I enjoy stargazing, camping, playing badminton, and volunteering.

Sylvia Chou

Secretary

Sylvia is double majoring in chemical engineering (biomolecular option) and chemistry. Her interests lie in research (particularly in inorganic chemistry with biomedical applications-especially carboranes!), baking chocolate chip cookies, and tallying up how many dimensionless numbers one can find throughout the third year of chemical engineering. Her plans for the far future involve doing research in academia or in industry, and therefore plans for the near future involve grad school.
